Reader Reading Notes

  • Summary after guided companion reading: This paper surveys Douyin service adoption among national, provincial, provincial-capital, and sub-provincial public libraries, then turns the survey into a service-development strategy. It finds that public-library account adoption was rising but still uneven, account influence was weak compared with large cultural institutions, original content was concentrated in library publicity/book recommendation/reading promotion/notices, and common problems included low opening rate, irregular management, broad or over-entertaining content, lack of professional teams, and weak online-offline interaction.
  • Innovation / value after guided companion reading: The paper is useful as a public-library service map. It moves beyond a general "library short video is useful" claim by sampling 63 public libraries and distinguishing adoption level, follower/like metrics, original-content categories, and service problems. It also provides one of the clearer strategy frames for public libraries: planning and goals, distinctive original content, PUGC production, integrated media operation, online-offline conversion, and cross-regional/cross-industry cooperation.
  • Method details after guided companion reading: The authors use Douyin Android v11.3.0 account search, take official verified accounts as the survey target, and collect account opening, likes, followers, works, posts, and first original-work time as of 2020-05-30. They use content analysis to categorize original works from public-library accounts.
