Reader Reading Notes

  • Summary after guided companion reading: This paper studies why some library Douyin videos become popular. It searches Douyin for library-related accounts, obtains 328 accounts from 320 libraries and 8,069 videos, then selects 103 non-forwarded videos with more than 1,000 likes for content analysis. It finds that more detailed titles, topic hashtags, subtitles, real-shot/interview formats, knowledge popularization and social-cultural content, celebrity/star appearance, and strong emotional color are associated with popular library short videos.
  • Innovation / value after guided companion reading: Its key value is a content-rule lens. Unlike broad status/problem papers, it narrows the object to "hot" library videos and codes their content characteristics. This makes it a useful bridge between descriptive operation studies and later influencing-factor models: it tells us which content features practitioners believed were visible in high-like cases before more formal regression/model papers appeared.
  • Method details after guided companion reading: The author searched Douyin in 2020-06 with keywords such as 图书馆, 省图书馆, 市图书馆, and 大学图书馆, recorded 328 library accounts and 8,069 videos, then used likes as the popularity gate. Videos with more than 1,000 likes were selected, forwarded videos from other creators were removed, and 103 videos were coded by duration, title length, hashtag/topic guidance, subtitles, video type, theme, emotion, and appearing roles.
