Reader Reading Notes

  • Summary after guided companion reading: This paper studies how public-library reading-service Douyin videos can achieve better platform communication effects. It collects videos from the National Library of China and 29 provincial public libraries, builds a video communication effect index (VCEI), selects 20 typical reading-service cases by VCEI percentile bands, and uses fsQCA with gatekeeper theory to identify multiple paths to strong communication effects. Its main contribution to library_short_video is moving from generic propagation-power metrics toward reading-service-specific effect mechanisms.
  • Project role: This is the next service-mechanism paper after cnki_2021_645f03f388. It helps fill the gap between 传播力评价 and 服务价值 by focusing on reading-service videos rather than account-level dissemination alone.
  • Method details after guided companion reading: The study uses Qingbo API data for 30 public-library Douyin accounts from 2018-08-03 to 2024-07-11, cleans 11,529 structured video records, defines VCEI from likes, comments, and shares using DCI-style interaction coefficients, identifies 6,350 reading-service videos with keywords such as 阅读, 悦读, 共读, 读书, 荐书, 书单, and 图书, selects 20 typical cases, calibrates fuzzy-set variables with 90%/50%/10% anchors, and analyzes configurations in fsQCA 4.0.
  • Core findings after guided companion reading: No single condition is necessary for ideal communication effect. Four configurations explain 52.5% of the result with overall consistency 0.986. The paths are summarized as video-presentation-and-background driven, video-presentation-and-content driven, and video-content-drainage synergy driven. Across all configurations, vivid video presentation is a recurring core condition.
