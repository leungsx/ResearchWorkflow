Reader Reading Notes

  • Summary after human reading: This paper builds a library-specific Douyin account dissemination-capability evaluation model for provincial public libraries. It starts from user, network, video, and dissemination characteristics, then retains five operational indicators: fan count, publication volume, video content quality, total likes, and hot-video index. The empirical object is 22 officially verified provincial public-library Douyin accounts, with data collected on 2020-10-30. The final ranking shows a very uneven field: Zhejiang Library ranks first, while 13 accounts score below 0.1.
  • Innovation / value after human reading: The main value is not a new causal theory, but a sector-adapted measurement attempt. Compared with generic DCI or Feigua-style platform indices, it tries to include library content quality and a corrected hot-video index, which is directly useful for the project's "propagation-power evaluation / service-value indicator" line.
  • Method details after human reading: The paper combines literature review, online account survey, expert/user scoring for content quality, indicator normalization, entropy weight method, weighted-sum scoring, six-level ranking, and Feigua-index comparison. The paper manually caps extreme fan and like values during normalization to reduce outlier domination, but those thresholds need to be treated as author decisions rather than universal rules.
